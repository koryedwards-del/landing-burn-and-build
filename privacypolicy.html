Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2026 | Kory Edwards | Tacoma, WA

Who We Are

Burn & Build is owned and operated by Kory Edwards, based in Tacoma, Washington. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at burnandbuilddiet.com.

Information We Collect

When you use the calculator on this site, the body stats you enter (gender, bodyweight, body fat percentage) are used only to generate your on-screen results. This data is not stored, transmitted, or linked to your identity.

When you use the Burn & Build app, onboarding and diet data are stored locally on your device. See the app privacy policy for full details.

We may collect standard technical information such as your browser type, IP address, and pages visited through cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to support requests and improve the website and program experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to understand how visitors interact with the site. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@burnandbuilddiet.com.
